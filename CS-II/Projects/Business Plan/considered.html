Business Plan - 6 Confused Men Corporation
We choose to endorse our own project because we feel it has a valid marketing strategy, has a target audience in mind, and has a problem that we provide a good solution to. Our project idea is to implement RFID scanners into entrances of schools so that the schools can see who has an ID when entering the school and who doesn’t. This ensures that people who don’t go to the school can’t enter undetected and that students without ID’s are caught. This would replace the security guards that usually check the ID’s. This would free up the labor and would ensure that people can’t just sneak into the building in a large crowd and remain undetected. The project also isn’t very costly because the RFID chips are very inexpensive and the scanners only need to be implemented in the entrances of the schools.
